How Does It Work?

The way the online world works right now is:

  1. You do stuff online like shopping, looking up stuff, paying bills, watching videos, etc
  2. All your data is collected to build a profile of you, like how old you are, male/female, interests, etc., by companies. This is done in a way where the companies doing it don't want you to be aware of what they are doing (it's a bit slimy)
  3. Your data is sold to businesses trying to advertise to you by these companies that collect your data
  4. You see adds that businesses pay for when you go online

Google, Facebook, etc make hundreds of billions of dollars doing this!

So, there are a few problems with this:

  1. You are getting ripped off
  2. This is a slimy practice only done because it makes so much money
  3. What if your data gets sold to a bad actor? Most people in the US, you can fairly quickly find their address, email address and emails, cell number and their current location (if they have their cell on them). Whether its your ex, some serial killer and just some government department, why allow them to have this info on you if you can fairly easily block it?

How the Internet should work regarding your data:

  1. You go online, do your thing
  2. As companies want your data, they try to get your data
  3. You have easy-to-use software blocking their evil software
  4. Your data is saved by the same software, encrypted with your password so no one can read it, not even us
  5. If you like, you can sell your data to businesses and receive most of the money businesses pay to advertise to you

We take all these people that have signed up or are using the software and go to businesses and get them to pay for advertising directly to consumer. Most of the money they pay goes to you.

Say you are a business owner and you are wondering if you should put a coffee shop in a certain place in the city. You could then buy the info of who likes coffee that is near your planned coffee shop as well as reach out via email, etc to invite them to your coffee store. Most of the money that is paid for this info goes to the users, a percent goes to this business.
